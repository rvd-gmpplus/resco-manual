üìñ Introduction

This manual contains information about the use of the GMP+ Audit app. The app can be used on laptop, tablet or mobile phone. Per device there may be some visual deviations from this manual, but functionality remains the same.

üí° Key Feature The GMP+ Audit app is available for both online and offline usage!

GMP+ Database vs. GMP+ Audit App

It's important to understand the difference between these two systems:

GMP+ Database GMP+ Audit App
Application of new companies Overview of planned/performed audits
Transfer certified companies to another CB Checklists per audit/review
Adding/changing certificates or company details General Certification Body information
Acceptation auditors (documentation) FSP Product list
Consult exam results, overview companies Follow up on audits
Planning of audits ‚Äî
For coordinators & admin staff For auditors & reviewers only

In case of any questions, the GMP+ Helpdesk can be contacted. Consult the GMP+ website for contact details.

‚ùå Non-Conformities (NC)

Adding a Non-Conformity

When creating an NC, the following fields are mandatory:

  • Compliance (classification)
  • Deadline NCR
  • Description of the Non-Conformity

Motivation auditor = Explain why the company does not comply with GMP+ rules and justify your classification choice.

üí° New Feature: Article Number You can now specify the exact article number for an NC. For example, if the checklist shows article 8.4, but the NC applies to article 8.4.1, you can enter "8.4.1" in the 'Article number NC' field.

Adding a Non-Conformity

Deleting a Non-Conformity

An NC can only be deleted when:

  • The NC was incorrectly entered
  • The audit is not yet finished (Audit Ready = 'No')

Deleting a Non-Conformity

Closing a Non-Conformity

To close an NC, fill in these fields:

  • NCR solved on (date)
  • Final classification
  • Corrective/preventive actions

Previous NC's

If an NC from a previous audit (same scope) was not closed, it will appear in the new audit checklist under "Non-conformities Previous Audit".

You have three options:

Option Description Result
Close NC is resolved, final classification is 'conform' NC is closed
Ignore NC will not be assessed during this audit NC appears in next audit (same scope)
Up-/Downgrade NC cannot be closed but needs reclassification New classification applies to next audit
‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Ignore is Permanent Once you set 'Ignore NC' to 'Yes', this cannot be changed back to 'No'!

Handling previous Non-Conformities

Up- or Downgrading an NC

When up- or downgrading a previous NC:

  1. Set 'Ignore NC' to 'No'
  2. Do NOT fill in 'NCR solved on' and 'Final classification'
  3. Select 'Downgrade / Upgrade'
  4. Fill in the required fields (shown in red)
  5. Save and return to the checklist

Up- or downgrading a Non-Conformity

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I cannot see any data in the NC report for Transport or Antibiotic free feed. Why?

Non-conformities for these scopes are not classified in minor, major or critical. All data is shown in the audit report instead. This can be signed by the shipper/company after the audit.

Q: How do I send a log file to GMP+ International?

  2. Click the icon in the right upper corner
  3. This prepares an email with the log file
  4. Include your email address in the subject line before sending
üí° Important Without your email address in the subject, GMP+ International won't know who sent the log file!

Sending log files and checking app version

## How to Update Screenshots

1. Open the Resco Mobile CRM app on your device
2. Navigate to the screen shown in the original screenshot
3. Take a new screenshot
4. Crop to show only the relevant UI elements
5. Save with the **same filename** to automatically replace

### Recommended Specifications

- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Resolution:** Match original or higher
- **Naming:** Keep existing filenames for automatic replacement
